Features:

Live-feed of all accounts

  1. Real-time data of your accounts: Banks, investments, pensions, credit cards, and crypto accounts
  2. Connect accounts from multiple countries
  3. Create custom widgets for your dashboard: split your information by country, account type, financial institution, or however you like

Transfer Money

  1. Balance your portfolio by transferring money between your accounts
  2. Set up automatic debits to your bank, investments, credit cards, and loan accounts
  3. Receive real time rates on FX conversions for GBP to EUR transfers

Net-worth Tracker

  1. Track your net-worth over time
  2. View the split between your equity and liabilities
  3. Filter accounts to include what you want

Portfolio Allocation

  1. View the allocation of your entire portfolio
  2. View the split between your equity and liabilities
  3. Filter accounts to include what you want

Investment accounts

  3. Get real time data of housing market values

  2. Secure and reliable connection every time
  3. Clean formatting for easy pivot and data refreshing
